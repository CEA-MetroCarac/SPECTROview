## 12. Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips

### Global Shortcuts
| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Ctrl + R` | Rescale spectra plot |
| `Ctrl + Click` on Copy | Copy numerical data to clipboard |
| `Mouse wheel` | Quick Y-axis rescale in Spectra Viewer |

### Tips
- **Tooltips everywhere**: Hover over any GUI element for 1 second.
- **Drag peaks**: Click and drag peaks directly in the Spectra Viewer.
- **Quick re-fit**: After adjusting parameters, click Fit again — it warm-starts from previous results.
- **Column names with spaces**: Use backticks (`` ` ``) in Filters or Computed Columns.
- **Profile extraction**: In Maps workspace, select exactly 2 points to extract an intensity profile.
