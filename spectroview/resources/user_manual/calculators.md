## **Quick Calculation Tool**

Click the **Calculator** icon in the `Menu Bar` to open a suite of utility calculators:

![Quick Calculation Tool](../user_manual_images/menu_bar/ui_quick_calculation.png)

### **1. Laser Spot Size, Depth of Field, and Laser Power Density**
This tool estimates the theoretical, diffraction-limited spatial resolution of your optical setup, including the spot size, depth of focus, and laser power density.

- **Spot Size**: Calculated as 1.22 × λ / NA (μm)
- **Depth of Focus**: Calculated as 4 × n × λ / NA<sup>2</sup> (μm)

### **2. Penetration Depth**
This tool calculates the theoretical optical penetration depth of a laser into a specific material, derived from its complex refractive index (extinction coefficient).

- **Penetration Depth (<i>d</i>)**: Calculated as λ / (4πk) (nm)

### **3. Unit Converter**
A rapid utility to convert between various standard spectroscopic units, ensuring seamless consistency during data analysis (e.g., converting wavelength in nm to energy in eV, or calculating Raman shift in cm<sup>-1</sup> from excitation wavelengths).
