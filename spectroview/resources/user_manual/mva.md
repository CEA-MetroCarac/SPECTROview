## Multivariate Analysis (MVA) 

(This is a holder page, the feature is under developpement, and the content is not completed yet)

SPECTROview features built-in Multivariate Analysis (MVA) tools, which are easily accessible from the dedicated **MVA tab** located within both the Spectra and Maps workspaces.
> **Note**: MVA algorithms require a minimum of 2 active spectra to run.

### Supported Algorithms

- **Principal Component Analysis (PCA)**: A statistical procedure that orthogonally transforms and reduces the dimensionality of complex spectral datasets, revealing the most significant patterns of variance.
  - **Available Visualizations**: Scree plots (variance explained), Principal Component Loadings, and Score plots.

- **Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF)**: A powerful decomposition algorithm that factors the spectral data into distinct, additive, non-negative components, often representing pure chemical endmembers.
  - **Available Visualizations**: Component Loadings and Score plots.
